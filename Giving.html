Giving

Each of us is called to worship God with our entire life, including our time, talents and resources. Sharing those resources is an important way to worship the Giver of all good things. We pledge to be good stewards of your gifts, taking great care in how we manage and track the resources entrusted to us.

Giving Methods
You may make your gifts in a variety of forms. You will receive a receipt of your tax-deductible gifts in accordance with IRS requirements.

Tokens
Many of us give of ourselves and our time. This is just as important of a gift as any monetary gift. During our Sunday morning worship we offer a variety of tokens that people may give during the offering. These tokens represent the time and talents that people give through out the week to Christ's Church.

Cash
You can give cash during Sunday morning worship services.

Checks or Money Orders
Checks or money orders can be given in two ways:

  • placed in the offering plate during Sunday-morning worship
  • mailed to Aloha Christian Church 2020 NW Aloclek Dr., Suite 121 Hillsboro, OR 97124

Online Giving
Aloha Christian Church offers the convenience and security of electronic, tax-deductible giving  for you to give one-time gifts or recurring donations by credit card (Visa/Master Card, American Express and Discover) or by electronic check either thru our Pay Pal or Google Pay gateways. To make an electronic check payment, you will need to provide the bank routing number and account number that appear at the bottom of a check. You may set up your offering to be weekly, bi-monthly, monthly, or in any manner you desire. This method is safe, consistent, and you won't have to worry about writing a check each week or mailing an offering in when you are away. Gifts can be designated for Aloha Christian Church General Fund, Youth Ministries or Outreach Ministries.

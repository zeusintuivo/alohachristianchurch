Youth & Education Ministries
Aloha Christian Church has a active Youth Ministry and Education program for children toddlers to high school. We strive to inspire and challange our youth to think and view the world through Christian eyes, deal with situations with Christian ethics, and instill Christian love and compassion on their hearts and minds.
